Vigour

Vigour is a free and open-source cross-platform app dedicated to helping its users develop fitness and maintain their health.

The purpose of Vigour is to provide a streamlined, simple, but highly customisable workout tracking experience. It is useful for weight training, running, sports, and most other physical activities that you might undertake.

What's different about Vigour?

Most workout tracking apps suffer from at least one of three problems:

  • They only address a particular style of workout, like running or weightlifting.
  • They restrict users to the set of workouts on their site, which are often inconsistent.
  • They lack any real ability for users to add workouts that they have done in the past, or even to add them in any way other than inputting them live as they do them. This is fine for a workout planner, but makes stats tracking woefully difficult.

Vigour attempts to address these problems using a unique framework that allows users to define their activities, how they record them, and what metrics they care about.
